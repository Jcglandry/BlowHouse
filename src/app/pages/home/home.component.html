The sound of Mobile Mardi Gras! Blow House Brass Band is a Second Line Jazz ensemble based out of Mobile, Alabama. Blow House has performed for revelers of all kinds at parades, balls, parties, and festivals since 2007. Described as "The Sound of Mardi Gras," Blow House plays a blend of classic Carnivale fare and down and dirty funk culminating in a unique and lively musical experience... just like the Azalea City we call home!
